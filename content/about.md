Hey! I'm **Mani Ratan Pratap Singh** (MRPS), based in India 🇮🇳.

My simplicity is my identity.

I'm a generalist by nature — I like to build things, photograph things, and understand how things work. This website is my permanent digital archive: a place where I collect my work, notes, and ideas over time.

## What I Do

- **Photography** — Night photography, long exposure, light trails. Currently shooting on a Canon EOS M50 Mark II with an EF 50mm f/1.8 STM.
- **Homelab** — Self-hosted infrastructure, networking, and home automation.
- **Electronics & Embedded** — ESP32 development, sensor nodes, IoT projects.
- **Robotics** — Robot builds and motor control experiments.
- **Networking** — VLANs, firewalls, DNS, VPNs, and generally making home networks too complicated.
- **AI** — Exploring large language models, local inference, and AI tooling.
- **Guitar & Audio** — Playing guitar and collecting too many effects pedals.
- **Travel** — Exploring India, one road trip at a time.
